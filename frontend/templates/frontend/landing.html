The Museum of Sound

Sounds shape the way we feel and see the world, yet we don't often stop and listen.

The Museum of Sound allows you to share the sounds you hear and tell the stories that come with them.

What is the Museum of Sound?

Do you remember the sound of dial-up internet? What about the whistle of an old kettle, the sound of the coffee machine at your favourite caf√©, the no-sound of a silent elevator, or a wine cork popping. All of these sounds are associated with emotions and memory.

The Museum of Sound allows you to share sounds, as well as sharing your stories about why these sounds are meaningful to you.

Get involved by recording a short sound clip on the 'Record a Sound' page and explaining why this sound matters to you.
